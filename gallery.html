Behind the Scenes Image & Video Gallery

This gallery is a collection of exclusive images and video clips captured during the making of Splatter Head. Here you'll find a variety of behind the scenes goodies such as: Characters under construction, the miniature sets taking shape, glimpses of the animation process in action, and maybe even some outtakes or tests that didn't make the final cut.

Consider this your visual diary of the Splatter Head production: Raw, unfiltered, and offering a unique look at the intricate, time consuming, and ultimately rewarding craft of claymation.
