Win a Chance to Be Animated as a Zombie!

Have you ever dreamed of seeing yourself immortalized on screen... as a gruesome, handmade claymation zombie? Well, now’s your chance to make that nightmare a reality! I'm hosting a special contest where one lucky winner will have their likeness transformed into a unique zombie character featured in my upcoming stop motion action horror video, Splatter Head. Imagine joining the horde and becoming a permanent part of this cheesy, clayfilled cinematic history!

How to Enter:

  1. Create Your Zombie Alter Ego: Unleash your inner undead! Draw a sketch, send a spooky selfie, or even film a short video showcasing your zombie persona. Go wild, get creative, make it gory, make it funny, make it cheesy – the more over the top, the better! Extra points for really committing to the look.
  2. Submit Your Entry: Send your masterpiece before the deadline of August 19, 2025, using the Contact Form on the Contact page.
  3. Include Required Info: Make sure to include your name, your zombie concept (link to image/video), and a short message explaining why YOU deserve to be reanimated in clay for Splatter Head.

Judging and Prize:

After submissions close, the top five finalists will be showcased online, and the Splatter Head fans will get to vote for their favorite! Voting will be open for approximately seven days.

The final winner will be chosen based on a combination of fan votes (50%) and my choice (50%), ensuring the winning design fits perfectly within the film's gruesome aesthetic. The grand prize? Your zombie design will be meticulously handcrafted in clay and featured front and center in the movie, plus you'll get a special behind the scenes feature showing your clay counterpart being built and animated!

Terms and Conditions

Before you submit your bid for undead immortality, please review the official rules of the Splatter Head Zombie Contest:

  • Eligibility: The contest is open to all fans worldwide, but you must be 18 years of age or older to participate.
  • Original Creation: Your submitted zombie concept (art, photo, or video) must be your own original idea. Please do not use copyrighted characters or artwork.
  • Usage Rights: By entering the contest, you agree that your zombie design can be adapted, modified, and used within the Splatter Head claymation project, as well as in related promotional materials and social media posts. I reserve the right to slightly modify the winning design to best fit the style and technical needs of the animation.
  • No Financial Compensation: Winning the contest grants you the unique prize of being featured in the project and receiving behind the scenes recognition, but there is no monetary payment or royalty involved. You do, however, gain eternal undead fame and bragging rights!
  • Respectful Submissions: All entries must be respectful. No offensive, hateful, or otherwise inappropriate content will be accepted.
  • Deadline: All entries must be received by the specified submission deadline (August 19, 2025).
  • Agreement: Submitting an entry signifies that you agree to all the contest rules and give permission for your entry to be showcased publicly during the finalist voting process.
